Introduction

Decentra Guild is an all-in-one platform for global collaboration. Our blockchain-based solution offers start-ups, small businesses, and groups a comprehensive suite of tools and services, including private tokens, secure vaults, multisig wallets, tax administration, and a legal framework. Whether you're looking to streamline your project or develop an open-source program, dGuild has everything you need to succeed.

Issues with Traditional Business Models

When we examine the current business models, several issues come to light. Traditional companies with stocks are often inflexible, slow to adapt, and expensive to alter. These models have their limitations, and it's time for a new approach.

Flawed Tokenomics in Web3 Space

Exploring the web3 space, we find that many projects suffer from flawed tokenomics, where tokens are simply released on a set schedule without regard for value added. The current approach to tokenomics has its drawbacks, and it's time for a more sustainable solution.

dGuild - Digital Co-ops

At dGuild - Digital Co-ops, we're committed to solving the problems inherent in traditional business models and flawed tokenomics. We've developed a double tokenomic system that allows projects to separate their financial investment from the time and effort required to complete them. Our platform provides a safe and sustainable solution for businesses, metaverse projects, and other web3 ecosystems.
