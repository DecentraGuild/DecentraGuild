What we offer

Welcome to DecentraGuild, the All-in-One Collaboration platform that aims to revolutionize global web3 collaborations. By combining the power of historical guilds with modern technology, we're opening up new possibilities for Gaming Guilds, art projects, and other web3 services to work together. Join us on this exciting journey as we bring together creators, innovators and community from around the world.

Our concept

Platform

Our platform provides a seamless and secure environment for collaboration. Discover a range of tools and features designed to streamline communication, project management, and resource sharing. Whether you're a developer, artist, or entrepreneur, our platform offers the infrastructure you need to turn your ideas into reality.

Ecosystems

Each ecosystem is unique and starts off as a private group with their own vaults, private tokens and multisig tree. Each ecosystem can manage itself by collaboration. We are providing a legal entity and framework that come with our tokenomic structure and vaults. Our web3 structure is fair to all involved parties and you know exactly what you get when you start. 

DAO and Multi-sig

Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) Voting puts the decision-making power in the hands of the community. Participate in the governance of a Decentra Guild by voting on important proposals, shaping the ecosystem's future, and ensuring a democratic and inclusive environment for all participants.

Dual Tokenomics

DecentraGuild operates on a dual token system. Both these tokens are functioning as Stable coins and each Vault holds the Security tokens (equity) of the entity. Together the circulating tokens have the power to utilize this equity and vote on important matters together, create value together and distribute through the tokens.

sKing Token

The sKing Token provides opportunities for individuals and organizations to Stake in the future of a DecentraGuild. By holding the sKing Token, you become a stakeholder in the ecosystem and benefit from their success. By staking your assets you give the ecosystem a passive income and a better chance of reaching their goals. 

wForce Token

The wForce Token is the lifeblood of DecentraGuild's ecosystem. It rewards creators, developers, and other contributors for their valuable contributions to projects and collaborations. Earn wForce Tokens by lending your skills, sharing resources, or supporting the community, and unlock a world of opportunities within our platform.

Vaults

Our secure Vaults offer a decentralized storage solution for your digital assets. Safeguard your creations, NFTs, in a tamper-proof environment. Rest easy knowing that your valuable assets are protected by cutting-edge encryption and distributed across the blockchain.

Manage your Ecosystem or Guild

All these little parts are spread amongst different dApps, we will bring them together on our platform, provide a solid tokenomic model and a legal framework that comes with that.

The following functions would be included

NFT gating

Harness the power of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) to create exclusive access and opportunities to participate to your dGuild Eco system. This NFT creates your Private community and unique experiences to your members, while leveraging blockchain technology for transparency and authenticity. 

Ranking System

Our NFTs track your performances inside the system and can unlock certain qualifications with in a Guild. We believe that doing the work makes you better at managing the work, so before you can apply for certain position in a eco system you need to meet the minimum requirements.

Social Intergration

Link your communication channels with your tokens, NFTs and community. 
Give access to different channels based on ranking or holding certain NFTs, get more engagement with your community by having them link their social account to their membership NFT.

DAO Voting

Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) Voting puts the decision-making power in the hands of the community. Participate in the governance by voting on important proposals, shaping the platform's future, and ensuring a democratic and inclusive environment for all participants.

Multi-sig

Security is paramount in the world of web3 collaborations. DecentraGuild supports multi-signature wallets, which require multiple parties to authorize transactions. This ensures enhanced protection against unauthorized access and adds an additional layer of trust to your collaborative endeavors.

Job Boards

Find opportunities and talent through DecentraGuild's job boards. Whether you're seeking skilled professionals or looking to contribute your expertise, our platform connects talent with exciting projects in the web3 space. All tasks will have rewards with WFT or a separate EXP token for members to rank up. 

Legal Framework

Gain insights into the legal framework underpinning DecentraGuild. Discover our commitment to compliance, data privacy, and regulatory standards, ensuring a secure and trustworthy environment for collaboration. With a solid foundation to work on and spread your wings together.

Administration

This will not be included in our MVP but is planned for later stages. The administrative tools and features available to guild leaders and project managers within Decentra Guild. Wallet based tax reports, based on your Investments in INT tokens and Earnings from WFT tokens
