Vaults

Vaults are decentralised smart contract wallets that are managed by the entire group or a big selected groups. The wForce and sKing vaults both work with their corresponding tokens and have the governance according the the Security Tokens inside the vault.
The dGuild vault is managed by verified members and a council that is voted by the community. Together they manage the main vault and distribute value through the chain of multisig wallets.
The end responsibility is with the task forces and their project managers to do the work according the pending tasks, distributing the assets assigned directly after verifying together for completion.

Proposals are more advance and can include multiple choice.

Vault

dGuild Vault

- Balance Sheet of DG
- Receives “the Burn”
- Only controlled and managed (not directly claimable)
- Spread across multiple layers of Multi-sig wallets
- On-chain administration (later stages)
- Holds no STO but would be able to hold NFTs or sKing
- Controls the membership NFTs

Vault

wForce Vault

- Rewards for members
- Receives “the Burn”
- Mint on ''distribution share'' only
- Claimable by wForce
- Starts with 100% of STO
- Holds governance because of the STO in vault
- Gives STO to sKing vault to increase sKing supply

Vault

sKing Vault

- Staking (passive income)
- Applies “the Burn” on initial stake
- Distribute on burn 70% sKing, 15% wForce , 15%dGuild 
- Mint by Members
- Claimable by sKing
- Receives STO in Vault to increase Circulation Cap sKing
- Receives governance by STO in vault

Vault

NFT funding Vault

- Fund the start of your Decentra Guild together
- 100% of vault is used to start a Ecosystem
- Mint by Founding members
- No governance or STO (Security token offering)
- The goal of Ecosystem is not profits but collaboration

Multi-Sig wallets

Multi-sig are like mini-vaults with smaller group handling a smaller portion of the value. We recommend having at least 5 people in each multi-sig. With each tier down, the number of multi-sigs can grow, this can be organically when there is a need. We want to create a easy tool for visualizing the multi-sig tree and help managing all permissions between them. 

The votes are ratter simple, its only Yes or No, you sign the transaction, and when enough people do this the event/transaction will be triggered. 

Multi-sig

dGuild multi-sig

- Balance Sheet of DG
- Receives the value of the entire Ecosystem
- Managed by the Council and the Lead managers
- Used to distribute the value towards sKing and wForce vaults
- On-chain administration (later stages)

Voting

At this page we learned that we use many types of voting inside our system, it could be with tokens or by signing a transaction in a multi-sig (give your consensus for that transaction). Its all baked into our system and gives the Ecosystem full control over their own situation

Vote

Assets moving

Use the collaborative power to move assets on-chain. This can be inside a taskforce for certain missions, Changing between departments, or even where you stake your Assets with a vault. 

Vote

Rule changing

Our platform give a wide set of variables that you can change by votes inside the community. As long as the changes stay within our template, your ecosystem is free to rule as they like. 

Vote

Permission inside Ecosystem

Your memberships are handled by vote as well, so a new member can only join your ecosystem when the assigned multi-sig completes the transaction. This NFT is also revokable if players are misbehaving. This would lock them out of your Ecosystem. 

Vote

Ranking permissions

Certain roles and information will be locked behind different NFTs too, This is because for certain permissions in the system like with the management of a dGuild vault you would need special verification and registration for example.
